The HSC-milling machine (high-speed cutting) holds the speed record among machine tools. The spindle rotation and feed speed is much higher with HSC-milling machines than with conventional milling machines so that an optimal material removal rate is achieved. Modern HSC machines are equipped with a powerful spindle motor, an automatic tool changer and a flexible CNC control (e.g. from HEIDENHAIN).
